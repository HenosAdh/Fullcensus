You are busy running a home. Here is everything I need from you, and when.

Twenty minutes. I ask about your home, your beds, and who you care for. That is the whole kickoff.

Day 2

Photos

Real photos of your home. Bright rooms, the yard, the table. This is the part families judge first.

Site, words, Google, pages, directory. You do nothing. Then you look at it and tell me what to change.

I cannot promise you a specific number of calls, tours, or move ins. Nobody honest can, and if someone does, be careful. What I can promise is this: your home will be findable when a family searches, it will load fast on a phone, the words will sound like you, and every account will be in your name. What happens after that is your care doing the work.
