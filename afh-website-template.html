About our home

Small, personal, and truly like family.

Cedarbrook is a licensed adult family home in Everett. We care for just a few residents at a time, so there's always real attention, familiar faces, and a day that moves at a comfortable pace.

We're a family. We cook the meals ourselves, we get to know each resident, and we look after your mom or dad the way we'd want our own parents looked after.
