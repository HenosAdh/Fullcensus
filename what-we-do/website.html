Why it matters for your home

Families decide in seconds.

When an adult daughter is looking for a place for her mother, she opens her phone, taps a few results, and forms an opinion of your home before she ever calls. If your home has no website, or a slow one that does not work on a phone, she moves on to the home that looks ready for her. You lose the tour before you knew it existed.

A clean, fast site puts your home on equal footing with the larger facilities she is also looking at. It shows your rooms, your care types, and how many beds are open right now, and it makes calling or messaging you take one tap. That is the difference between a bed that sits empty and a bed that gets a tour booked this week.

Because most families start online and judge a home in seconds, a fast, real website earns their trust right away, so more of them call and book a tour.

Is a website worth it?

One filled bed pays for it.

One filled bed is worth about $5,000 a month. So the very first family your website brings in more than pays for the site. After that, every family it brings is added income.

The site is yours. You own it and keep it for as long as you run your home. It keeps working for you year after year.

If you run two homes, each home needs its own site. Families search by city, and one site cannot show up at the top for two cities at once. A site for each home means each one can be found.
