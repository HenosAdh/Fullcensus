What we do

Get on the right desks.

Rack cards and brochures for discharge planners, social workers, and case managers, the people who refer most placements. We design and print them so your home sits on the right desks, not only online.

Why it matters for your home

Most placements come from a referral.

A large share of the families who fill adult family homes never find you on their own. They are pointed to you by a hospital discharge planner, a social worker, or a case manager who is handing a worried family a short list of places to call. Those professionals decide fast, often with a stack of brochures on the desk in front of them. If your home is not in that stack, you are not on the list, no matter how good your website is.

A clean brochure and rack card put your home in their hands. They give a discharge planner something to pass to a family on the spot, with your photos, your care types, and your number right there. Online presence brings in the families who are searching themselves. Print is how you reach the people who send you the rest, and it is the part most homes skip entirely.

Because many families are guided to a home by a discharge planner or case manager, having your brochure and rack card on their desk means your home gets named when a bed opens up.
